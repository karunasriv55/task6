<h1> Fixed numbers</h1>
<p>ToFixed() converts a number to a string, rounded to a specified number of decimals:</p>

<p id="demo"></p>

<script>
let num = 5.56789;
let n = num.toFixed();

document.getElementById("demo").innerHTML = n;
</script>
